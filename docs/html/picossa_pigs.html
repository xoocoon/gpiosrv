picossa_pigs module¶

Classes for provding a pigs interface on top of gpiosvr.picossa. pigs refers to the exceutable of the same name of the pigpio project.

With an instance of PigsExecutor, it is possible to execute commands and scripts like:

m 12 w pud 12 o w 12 1

This sets GPIO 12 as an output GPIO, disables the internal resistor and sets it to level 1.
